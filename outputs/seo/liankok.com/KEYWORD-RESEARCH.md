## Executive Summary

**Business type:** Hybrid local electrical distributor + B2B/B2C product catalogue (Singapore)
**Total keywords identified:** 72 (18 primary, 24 long-tail, 12 PAA, 12 AI search, plus 6 navigational excluded)
**Top 3 opportunities:**

1. **ABB authorised distributor Singapore** — Lian Kok is the only player who can own this phrase outright. ElectGo claims "authorized distributor" but lacks Lian Kok's 35-year exclusive partnership. Low difficulty, medium volume, zero direct replication possible.
2. **ABB S200 MCB Singapore** — Factomart and ElectGo both rank here with dedicated pages. Lian Kok has no page targeting this despite being the authorised distributor. A properly structured product page + specification guide would outrank both.
3. **MCB price Singapore / ABB MCB price Singapore** — ElectGo has a dedicated price guide page ranking for this. Lian Kok has no price content at all. This is a high-intent informational/transactional query that directly drives purchase enquiries.

**Competitor keyword coverage gaps confirmed (live):**
- ElectGo: ABB brand hub, MCB price guide, ABB MCB specifications guide, RCD category, RCBO category
- Lim Kim Hai: ACB/MCCB/MCBs distributor page, RCCB/RCBO dedicated page
- Factomart: ABB circuit breakers category, ABB S200 MCB model pages, downloadable price list/catalogue
- RS Singapore: Generic brand/category ranking (MCBs, RCCBs) — multi-brand, not ABB-specific

**Critical gap:** Lian Kok has no resource content (guides, price references, specifications) despite being the most authoritative ABB source in Singapore. All educational keyword territory is ceded to ElectGo and Factomart.

## Competitive Landscape

### ElectGo (electgo.com)
**Keyword strategy:** Multi-brand breadth + ABB depth. Targets every product category with dedicated pages, then layers resource content (price guides, specification guides) to capture informational queries.
- Ranks for: "ABB authorized distributor Singapore", "MCB Singapore", "ABB MCB price guide", "RCBO Singapore", "RCD Singapore"
- Content assets: ABB brand hub, MCB price guide, ABB MCB specs guide, product category pages for every circuit protection type
- Weakness: Generalist — carries 70,000+ products, not ABB specialists. No physical counter. Cannot claim authorised distributor exclusivity.

### Lim Kim Hai Electric (limkimhai.com.sg)
**Keyword strategy:** Category pages for each product type, multi-brand positioning.
- Ranks for: "ACB MCCB MCBs distributor Singapore", "RCCB RCBO Singapore"
- Weakness: Pivoting away from product distribution to "Beyond MRO" consulting — topical gap opening up in their ABB and electrical distribution content.

### Factomart Singapore (factomart.com.sg)
**Keyword strategy:** Model-specific ABB pages with downloadable price lists.
- Ranks for: "ABB S200 MCB Singapore", "ABB circuit breaker Singapore", "ABB MCB price list"
- Weakness: Not an ABB authorised distributor. Price list is a lead magnet, not authoritative pricing. No guides or educational content.
- **Note:** Factomart was not in the original audit — flagging as a direct competitor for ABB product search queries.

### RS Singapore (sg.rs-online.com)
**Keyword strategy:** Global authority, multi-brand, fast delivery angle.
- Ranks for: Generic "MCBs Singapore", "RCCBs Singapore" — but not ABB-specific
- Weakness: Not local, not authorised, premium pricing, no trade counter.

### Most Significant Gaps
1. No competitor has a comprehensive "ABB authorised distributor Singapore" explanation page — Lian Kok can own the definition
2. No competitor combines ABB pricing + local availability + trade counter in one resource
3. Factomart's ABB S200 page is product-only — no installation guidance, specifications, or Singapore-specific context
4. LKH's pivot away from distribution is opening up their "electrical distributor Singapore" keyword territory
