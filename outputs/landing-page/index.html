The Problem

Most SME founders are running their business in the dark.

Important information about the business is scattered everywhere. Conversations on WhatsApp. Finances in accounting software. Operations in spreadsheets. Meetings across multiple platforms.

Every piece of the business exists somewhere. But nowhere does the entire picture exist together. So every day, founders spend their time doing something they should never need to do — they search for answers.
