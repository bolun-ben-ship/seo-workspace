## Organization Overview

**RightClick:AI** — an AI dev shop being built for AI consultants.

The business designs and implements AI operating systems: connecting a client's data, automating operational workflows, and eventually deploying an AI layer that gives founders real-time visibility and strategic intelligence across their entire business — so they stop being the bottleneck.

The current pivot is from "SME service businesses broadly" → **AI consultants specifically** as the primary client. The belief: AI consultants already understand the value of AI systems, have technically sophisticated clients, and are better positioned to buy and deploy what RightClick:AI builds.

## Products / Services / Focus Areas

**Core offer: The AI Operating Layer** — built in 3 stages:

1. **Stage 1 — Operational Visibility**: Centralise business data (CRM, financials, meeting notes, communication channels, internal workflows) into a single environment. The founder can now understand what's happening across the business without asking their team.

2. **Stage 2 — Growth Automation**: Once visibility exists, automate the operational workflows: lead handling, client onboarding, internal coordination, reporting, follow-ups. Operations become faster and less people-dependent.

3. **Stage 3 — AI COO**: The system becomes a strategic intelligence layer. It continuously analyses business performance, operational bottlenecks, customer behaviour, and market trends — surfacing answers to questions like "What deals are stuck?" and "Where are we losing revenue?" so the founder can lead instead of manage.

**Adjacent offers being considered:**
- "Learn process thinking + gather data + build AI automations" — teachable methodology
- "How to think like an AI consultant" — positioning/training for consultants themselves

## Target Clients

**Primary (current pivot):** AI consultants — individuals and small firms who advise businesses on AI adoption. They understand AI, have clients who need implementation, and are a natural distribution channel.

**Secondary (original positioning):** Consulting businesses and service-based SMEs — specifically:
- Accounting firms
- Management consultancies
- Education businesses
- Immigration firms
- Operationally complex SMEs

**Ideal client profile:**
- Founder-led, 5–50 person service business
- Operationally scattered (data in WhatsApp, spreadsheets, multiple platforms)
- Founder or the chief operator is the decision bottleneck
- Has grown to a point where adding headcount isn't solving the coordination problem
- Thinks empirically, values data, wants clarity over noise

**Not ideal:**
- Businesses wanting a quick "copy-paste" automation fix
- Tool-first thinkers looking for a tech vendor, not a strategic partner
- Decision makers who do not understand AI technology, process thinking or do not value data

## Key Method / IP

**The Data Process Thinking Method:**
- Audit the process before touching any tool
- Map Input > Output at every stage
- Surface invisible micro-processes
- Identify what data exists and what decisions it can support
- Only then design and build the automation layer

The philosophy: most AI automation failures happen before the first tool is chosen. The bottleneck is clarity of process, not capability of technology.
