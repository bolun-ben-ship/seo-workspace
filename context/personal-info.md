## Unique Method

**The Data Process Thinking Method** — before any automation, you audit the process:
- Input > Output: what goes in where, what comes out at what stage
- Expenditure vs ROI: what do you put in, what do you get out
- Process mapping: surface the invisible micro-processes
- Data: what do we actually know, and can we make calculated decisions from it

One overlooked logical detail can break an entire system. This is the core belief behind the method.
