## E-E-A-T Strategy

Sleep/back pain content is YMYL-adjacent — E-E-A-T is weighted heavily by Google.

**Experience**
- Feature real customer stories with photos and back pain context on product pages
- Make the founder story (personal back pain) prominent on About page and product pages
- Show the showroom: photos, team, in-use lifestyle imagery

**Expertise**
- All blog posts need a named author with a short bio (sleep wellness background minimum; physiotherapy credentials ideal)
- Partner with a Singapore physiotherapist or sleep clinic for "Reviewed by" credits on back care content
- Cite relevant sleep research where possible

**Authoritativeness**
- Get featured in at least 5 Singapore mattress roundups (sethlui, thesmartlocal, bestinsingapore, weavvehome, modernhome.sg)
- Leverage Lazada #1 status and CertiPUR-US certification visibly on-site
- Pursue media coverage of the back pain angle (PR hook: "Singapore's #1 back care mattress brand")

**Trustworthiness**
- Prominent display: CertiPUR-US badge, 100-night trial terms, 24-hour service, Lazada #1
- Verified on-site reviews with aggregate star rating (Judge.me or Okendo)
- Clear privacy policy, returns policy, and contact details
- Google Business Profile fully completed and actively maintained

## Technical Priorities (in dependency order)

1. Fix Cloudflare bot access — everything depends on this
2. Verify robots.txt — ensure no unintentional blocks
3. Submit sitemap to GSC — accelerate indexation
4. Resolve `/en/` URL prefix — standardise or configure hreflang correctly
5. AggregateRating schema — install reviews app, import reviews, activate schema
6. LocalBusiness schema — showroom local SEO
7. Core Web Vitals — audit via PageSpeed Insights, fix top 3 issues
8. Organization schema — brand entity building
9. ProductGroup schema for size variants — Google Merchant Center eligibility
10. `llms.txt` — AI search visibility (Month 9+)
